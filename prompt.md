## SEO Requirements (Critical)

### Primary Keywords to Target
- Custom stamps Delhi
- Rubber stamp maker in Delhi
- Self-inking stamps Delhi
- Personalized stamp maker Shakarpur
- Custom rubber stamp manufacturing

### Secondary Keywords to Target
- Date stamps, Embossing seals, Name plates Delhi, Common seals, Business stamp solutions, Bulk stamp orders, Corporate stamping services, Steel brass plates Delhi

### Local SEO Keywords
- Shakarpur Delhi, Vikas Marg Delhi, Computer Market Delhi, East Delhi, North East Delhi, Delhi NCR

### Page Title Format
- Structure: `[Primary Service] | [Location] | Krishan Stamps Maker`
- Example: `Custom Rubber Stamps | Delhi | Krishan Stamps Maker`
- Length: 50-60 characters maximum

### Meta Description Format
- Must include: primary keyword, value proposition, call-to-action
- Example: `Get custom rubber stamps in Delhi. Fast turnaround, competitive pricing. Order online or visit our Shakarpur shop.`
- Length: 150-160 characters maximum

### Header Tag Structure
- **H1:** One per page only (main value proposition or page topic)
- **H2:** Service categories, product types, major sections (About, Testimonials, etc.)
- **H3:** Specific products, features, customization options, subsections

### Image Optimization
- All images must have descriptive, keyword-rich alt text
- Format: `[Product description] - Krishan Stamps Maker Delhi`
- Example: `Custom rubber stamp with company logo - Krishan Stamps Maker Delhi`

### Internal Linking Strategy
- Homepage → All product/service pages → Contact page
- Product pages → Related services and products
- All pages → Contact page
- FAQ → Relevant service pages

### Schema Markup Requirements
- Implement LocalBusiness schema on all pages
- Include: business name, address, phone, email, opening hours, geo coordinates, rating
- Product schema for individual product pages
- FAQ schema for FAQ page

### NAP Consistency
- Identical business information (Name, Address, Phone) must appear on every page
- Footer: Full NAP + email + business hours
- Contact page: Prominent NAP display with schema markup
- Header/Navigation: Phone number with click-to-call functionality
